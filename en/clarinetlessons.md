## Clarinet Lesson Overview

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Target age | Under 40 |
| Format | 1:1 online lessons (Zoom) |
| Lesson duration | 60/75 minutes per session |
| Tuition | 60 minutes: $70<br>75 minutes: $80|
| Level | Beginner to lower-intermediate (youth orchestra preparation available) |
| Materials | Sheet music and practice guides (PDF); video feedback when needed |

### Who is this for?
- Learners who want to build solid clarinet fundamentals
- Students preparing for school ensembles, youth orchestras, auditions, or entrance exams
- Learners who need guidance on posture, breathing, embouchure, or finger technique

### Method books
- Langenus Method Book, Vol. 1

### Lesson format
- Initial level assessment and goal setting
- Weekly assignments with feedback
- Video-based feedback provided when necessary for posture and sound production

### What you need
- Clarinet, reeds/reed case, swab, cork grease
- A metronome app
- A device for Zoom (external microphone recommended)

### Who is this for?
- Learners who are new to German
- Students preparing for applications to German music or art universities
- Learners preparing for exchange programs, job applications, or audition interviews
- Students preparing to transfer to Korean–German schools or elementary schools in Germany

- telc Deutsch A2–B2
- Goethe-Zertifikat Deutsch A2–B2

- 1:1 or 1:2 online lessons
